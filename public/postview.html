White Water Rafting

Red Rocks, Colorado Jaxon Hoffman

Fair warning, rafting in Colorado is not on the cheap spectrum. It will cost about $75 per person. I got to enjoy different grades of rapids so I could both experience the nature around me and have an exhilarating time. You will have a guide with you to help navigate the rapids.
